03 — Pirate's Code

The Pirate's Code

01

⚔️ Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect. No bullying, harassment, or hate speech of any kind. Every crewmate deserves to feel safe aboard this ship.

02

🌊 Rules for Spamming

We know spamming is fun — that's why we have a dedicated channel for it! Spam only in the spam channel. Spamming anywhere else may result in a mute or ban.

03

🚫 No NSFW Content

Keep the server safe for everyone. No inappropriate, explicit, or graphic content anywhere in the server.

04

🗺️ Use the Correct Channels

Post things in the correct channel to keep the ship organized and easy to navigate for the whole crew.

05

📢 No Advertising Without Permission

Do not promote other servers, channels, or products unless a mod explicitly allows it. Unsolicited ads will walk the plank.

06

💬 Use of Abusive Words

Using offensive words to disrespect someone will not be tolerated. However, using them casually to express yourself (not directed at others) is fine.

07

🛡️ Listen to Moderators

If a mod or admin asks you to stop something, please listen. Their word is law aboard this ship — handle disagreements through DMs, not public drama.

08

🔗 No Scams or Fake Links

Do not send suspicious links or attempt to scam, phish, or deceive other crewmates. Traitors get banned.

09

🔒 Respect Privacy

Do not share personal information — yours or anyone else's. What's shared aboard the ship stays aboard the ship.

10

🎉 Have Fun!

Enjoy the server and be friendly to everyone. This is your hangout spot — make the most of it, Nakama!
